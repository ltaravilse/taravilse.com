Results in Mathematical Olympiads

  • Iberoamerican Mathematical Olympiad for University Students 2015: Silver Medal (Link to results). I had the score for a achieving a Gold Medal (I scored 20 points, 18 was the minimum for Gold) but only one Gold Medal is awarded per country.
  • Mathematical Competition for University Students in Argentina (CIMA) 2014: 4th place in team with Agustín Santiago Gutiérrez (Link to results).
  • Mathematical Competition for University Students in Argentina (CIMA) 2013: 6th place in team with Agustín Santiago Gutiérrez (Link to results).
  • Mathematical Competition "Ernesto Paenza" 2010: 8th place and Honorable Mention in team with Nicolás Ponieman (Link to results).
  • Mathematical Competition "Ernesto Paenza" 2009: 9th place and Honorable Mention in team with Agustín Santiago Gutiérrez (Link to results).
